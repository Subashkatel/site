For larger equations, use double dollar signs:

$$
\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx = F(b) - F(a)
$$

You can also write multi-line equations:

$$
\begin{aligned}
f(x) &= x^2 + 2x + 1 \\
&= (x + 1)^2 \\
&= (x + 1)(x + 1)
\end{aligned}
$$
